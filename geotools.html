Instruments of Matter

Alongside research collection and material investigation, I will be producing a Geo-tool. This will be an instrument and physical device that measures and collects primary Earth information. The idea is to create an observation technology with creative expression. While developing this tool, a set of inputs will be defined with a focus on the geological shifts in the landscape. In terms of outputs I will explore the potentials of non-linear aesthetics and languages. In previous projects this was sound (noise) but for this development year I would like to explore other media. This Geo-tool will vary in design and be shaped for purpose.
